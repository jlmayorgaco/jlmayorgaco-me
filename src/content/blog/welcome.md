Welcome to the JLMT Lab blog! This is where I'll be sharing technical explorations, research insights, and engineering observations from my work in robotics, distributed control systems, and embedded systems.

## What to Expect

Here you'll find a mix of:

- **Research Notes**: Deep dives into control theory, multi-agent systems, and signal processing
- **Technical Tutorials**: Step-by-step guides on FPGA development, robotics, and embedded systems
- **Project Updates**: Progress reports and lessons learned from ongoing experiments
- **Engineering Thoughts**: Musings on software architecture, system design, and the craft of engineering

## The Lab Environment

This blog is part of a larger "cyber-physical lab interface" I'm building. You can explore:

- **Projects**: Technical implementations and hardware builds
- **Papers**: Academic publications and research
- **Data Lab**: Interactive experiments and visualizations
- **Research**: In-depth technical notes and analyses

## Let's Connect

I'm always interested in collaborating on robotics, control systems, and embedded engineering projects. Feel free to reach out through the contact page or connect with me on social media.

Stay curious, keep building.
