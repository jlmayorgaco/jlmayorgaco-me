abstract: "This paper presents a novel approach to real-time distributed control in multi-robot systems using a peer-to-peer communication architecture. We demonstrate sub-millisecond latency across heterogeneous robot platforms without relying on a central coordinator. Our implementation leverages ESP32 modules with custom MAC-layer protocols to achieve deterministic communication schedules. Experimental results on a fleet of 10 mobile robots show 99.9% task completion rate with average coordination overhead of 2.3ms."
