abstract: "This paper presents a comprehensive benchmarking framework for evaluating dynamic frequency estimators in low-inertia power systems with inverter-based resources (IBR). We evaluate multiple estimation methods including IpDFT, SRF-PLL, SOGI-FLL, EKF/UKF/RA-EKF, Koopman-based approaches, and machine learning estimators under realistic stress scenarios. Our methodology focuses on the latency vs robustness trade-off, identifying hidden failure modes and establishing performance boundaries for each technique."
