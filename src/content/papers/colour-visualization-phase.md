abstract: "This paper introduces a novel method for visualizing the phase of complex signals using color representation. By mapping phase angles to a perceptually uniform color space, we enable intuitive interpretation of phase information in signals such as ECG, speech, and vibration analysis. The proposed visualization technique preserves phase continuity and provides real-time rendering capabilities suitable for interactive analysis tools."
