Nolan's most human film. Cillian Murphy's performance is all quiet intensity and haunted looks. The non-linear structure works better than expected - the security hearing and the Trinity test build toward each other.

The IMAX presentation is overwhelming by design. You feel the physics happening. The sound design during the Trinity test - that long silence before the shockwave - is Nolan at his best.

What stays with you is the weight of it all. Oppenheimer's quote "Now I am become Death, destroyer of worlds" isn't triumphant, it's a realization of horror. The final scene with Einstein is perfect.

**Watched because:** I'm a physicist by training. The scientific accuracy matters, and they got the core physics right.
