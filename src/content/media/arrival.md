The best first contact movie. Not about lasers and explosions, but about communication and understanding. Amy Adams' performance is understated and devastating.

The heptapod language design is brilliant - circular symbols that imply time is non-linear. The Sapir-Whorf hypothesis taken to its logical extreme. When you understand the twist, everything recontextualizes.

Jóhann Jóhannsson's score (his last before his death) is otherworldly. The vocalizations for the heptapods still give me chills.

**For control systems people:** The way Louise maps the language, finds patterns, builds understanding - it's not unlike reverse-engineering a protocol or control algorithm.
