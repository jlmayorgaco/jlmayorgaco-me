Work-life balance taken to its absolute extreme. The concept of surgically separating work memories from personal memories is terrifying in its plausibility. The cinematography is clinical and cold, perfect for the sterile office environment. The pacing is deliberately slow but never boring - every scene builds tension.

What resonates most is how it critiques corporate culture while being completely absurd. The " Macrodata Refinement" department doing meaningless work feels too real. The second season is finally expanding the world beyond Lumon.

**Favorite episode:** "The You You Are" (S1E8) - the revelation scene is masterfully done.
