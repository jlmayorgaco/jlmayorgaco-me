Dramatized but entertaining look into Formula 1. Yes, they manufacture rivalries and edit radio messages out of context. But as a behind-the-scenes view of the sport, it's unmatched.

The engineering is the real star. Watching the teams iterate designs, optimize aero, manage tires - it's high-speed R&D with billion-dollar budgets. The cost cap has made it more interesting strategically.

Christian Horner and Guenther Steiner are reality TV gold whether you like them or not. The access Netflix gets is the real value here - pit lane, strategy meetings, driver briefings.

**As an engineer:** The technical regulations episodes are the best. Watching Adrian Newey explain ground effect aerodynamics is worth the subscription alone.
